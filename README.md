
# Super Store Sales Dashboard

## Overview

The Super Store Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, shipping trends, and regional performance. The dashboard provides actionable business insights through dynamic visualizations, KPI tracking, and interactive filtering, enabling data-driven decision-making.

## Business Objective

The objective of this project is to monitor and evaluate key sales metrics, identify profitable products and regions, understand customer purchasing behavior, and optimize business operations through comprehensive sales analytics.

## Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales: 522K
* Total Orders: 7,298
* Total Profit: 68K
* Average Delivery Time: 4 Days

### Sales Analysis

* Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
* Year-over-Year Sales Comparison
* Sales Performance by Category
* Sales Performance by Sub-Category

### Customer Analysis

* Sales Distribution by Customer Segment
* Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office Analysis

### Regional Analysis

* Sales by Region
* State-wise Sales and Profit Analysis using Map Visualization

### Shipping Analysis

* Sales by Ship Mode
* Delivery Performance Insights

### Payment Analysis

* Sales Distribution by Payment Mode
* COD, Online, and Card Payment Analysis

## Key Insights

* Consumer segment generated the highest share of sales.
* Technology category contributed significantly to overall revenue.
* Standard Class was the most frequently used shipping mode.
* Several states consistently outperformed others in sales and profit generation.
* Sales demonstrated steady growth across multiple months, indicating positive business performance.
